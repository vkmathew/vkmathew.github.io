Over the years, I have specialized in building, sustaining, and troubleshooting large-scale NAS and SAN storage infrastructures, working across platforms such as Dell PowerScale (Isilon), EqualLogic, Compellent, NetApp, and EMC VNX.

My expertise spans deep-dive debugging, RCA analysis, performance tuning, and data migrations with proven success in reducing downtime, improving test efficiency, and delivering fixes that consistently achieve 85%+ C-SAT scores.
